The Challenge

Most 3PLs are running on spreadsheets and legacy tools

WMSPro replaces the chaos with a purpose-built platform designed for how South Indian 3PLs actually operate.

Pain Points

Inventory Discrepancies

Manual counts, lost stock, client complaints during audits no single source of truth.

Billing Errors

Complex 3PL billing storage, handling, value-adds done manually on Excel, prone to revenue leakage.

No Serial / Batch Visibility

Can't track serialized goods or batches across GRN storage dispatch. Compliance risk.

No Client Transparency

Clients call and email for stock updates. No self-service portal. High support burden.

WMSPro Solution

Real-time Stock Visibility

Live inventory dashboard per client, per SKU, per location. Discrepancies caught instantly.

3PL-Native Billing Engine

Auto-calculate storage, handling, and value-added services per client contract. Zero revenue leakage.

Serial & Batch Tracking

Full traceability from inbound GRN to outbound dispatch. Barcode and RF scanner ready.

Client Self-Service Portal

Each client gets their own view stock levels, pending orders, billing history. Zero support calls.

Core Modules

Everything your warehouse needs, built in

Six purpose-built modules covering every warehouse operation from gate-in to billing.

Goods Receipt Note (GRN)
Capture inbound shipments with serial/batch tracking, damage recording, and client-wise SKU mapping. Full GRN workflow from PO to putaway.
Serial Tracking Barcode Multi-client
Delivery Orders
Create, manage and dispatch delivery orders with pick-list generation, packing verification, and dispatch confirmation. Full outbound workflow.
Pick & Pack Auto-Dispatch
Gate In / Gate Out
Manage vehicle movement, driver details, material entry/exit at the gate. Complete dock management with timestamped logs.
Vehicle Log Dock Management
Stock Visibility
Real-time inventory across all clients, SKUs, and storage zones. Aging stock alerts, slow-mover analysis, and bin-level visibility.
Live Dashboard Aging Alerts Bin Level
Billing Engine
3PL-native billing for storage, handling, value-added services, and labeling. Contract-based rates per client with auto-invoice generation.
Contract Rates Auto Invoice Multi-service
Client Portal
Dedicated portal per client view their stock, download dispatch reports, raise queries, and track orders. White-labeled access available.
Self-Service White Label
Also includes Labor Management track manpower utilization, task assignments, shift performance, and productivity metrics per warehouse.
